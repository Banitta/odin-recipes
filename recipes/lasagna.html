Vegetable Lasagna

Description

This recipe is for a super rich vegetarian lasagna. You can choose to follow the vegetable ingredients listed here, or substitute for other vegetables that you like.

To make the dish vegan, you can use vegan cheeses and use a cashew mixture as a substitute for ricotta. Also, to make it healthier, you can use eggplant or zucchini in place of lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pack of lasagna noodles
  • 32 oz jar of tomato sauce
  • Seasoning and spices of your choice (garlic powder, italian seasoning, etc.)
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 zucchinis
  • 1 bag of spinach
  • 2 cups mozarella cheese
  • 2 cups or parmesian cheese
  • 16 oz part-skim ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit
  2. In a large pot of boiling water, cook lasagna noodles according to package directions for al dente
  3. Saute onion, zucchini, and spices in a large sauce pan
  4. Add tomato sauce to sauce pan and simmer for about 15 minutes
  5. In a separate bowl, mix spinach with 1 cup of mozarella cheese and the part skim ricotta cheese.
  6. Once everything is cooked and ready, layer your lasagna noodles in the following sequence:
    1. Tomato sauce mixture
    2. Lasagna noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Parmesian cheese
  7. Once you lasagna pan is filled to the top, add any leftover cheese and the remaining 1 cup of moxarella cheese to the top of the dish.
  8. Cook the lasagna in the oven for 45 minutes and then observe the top of your dish to see if the cheese has browned. If not, cook for an additional 10-15 minutes. Let the dish cool for at least 10 minutes before you serve.
