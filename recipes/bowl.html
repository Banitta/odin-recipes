Warm Keto Bowl

Description

This warm bowl recipe is an attempt to create a savory, filling dish that is also keto friendly. You can choose to make this vegan by using an alternative protein instead of chicken. Bowls should contain an assortment of vegetables and proteins with optional dairy or grain slections. Honestly, bowls, can be filled with any food combination that you like eating!

Ingredients

  • Cooked Greens (spinach, kale, bok choy)
  • Diced bell peppers (for color use red, yellow, and orange)
  • 1 Diced Onion
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • Zuchinni spirals (zoodles)
  • 3 oz of cooked and seasoned chicken
  • 2 tbsp of your favorite hummus

Steps

  1. Saute your green vegetables, bell peppers, onion, minced garlic, and zoodles.
  2. Add seasoning of your choice to your cooked vegetables.
  3. Add your cooked chicken to the pan and lower the heat. cook for 3 minutes
  4. Put all your ingredients in a bowl add some hummus and enjoy!
